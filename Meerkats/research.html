Reason research is needed



Research is needed to learn more about the unknown center of the Earth to better understand what Earth's core is made up. Right now, scientists have only speculated what the deepest part of Earth's makeup is, but with GPS and video on the meerkats, we can finally see Earth's core for the first time. Researcher also believe with the knowledge of the Earth’s core it can give answers to how Earth was formed, which is a question that has been baffling scientist for centuries.


Mariana Trench Meerkats have unique identifiers



Meerkats are unique like Orcas in they have a one of a kind identifiers on their fur coats. All the meerkat's images were captured on the submarines camera system, which has been run through our AI system, where they were measured with millions of points and given an identifier. No two meerkats have the same id, and researchers have stated that they can tell the differences with the human eye relatively quickly.
