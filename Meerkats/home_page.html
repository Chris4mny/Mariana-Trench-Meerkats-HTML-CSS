Mariana Trench Meerkats









Mission Statement

Our mission is to explore all ends of the ocean to better understand our ecosystem. The discovery of the Meerkats will better assist us with exploration into areas that are impossible for humans to enter. By utilizing the Meerkats, we want to be able to prove or disprove what we have thought about Earth's core, and possibly discover new species that may have never been found.

About our research team

Our research team is utilizing the best technology to explore the depth of the ocean. It is made up of many different fields of scientists such as marine biologists, zoologists, botanists, and a former astronaut who is a physicist. The team now could utilize the discovery of the Mariana Meerkats to investigate unknown territories below the oceans floor.

Clyde Moon “The discovery of the Meerkats has given us an opportunity that can answer questions about the earth, and without their help, we may never know what lies beneath.”

They utilize the galaxy 8000 submarine to go to previously impossible depths. The galaxy 8000 can sustain depths of ten miles, holds a crew of thirteen people, and can stay underwater for three weeks before having to resurface.
